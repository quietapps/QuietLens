Follows Apple HIG conventions. Buttons + inputs share the 8pt radius for consistent edges (HIG bordered-control geometry). Cards step up to 12. Window chrome & popovers = 14 (macOS sheets & menus). Modals/sheets = 22 (matches macOS sheet corner). App icons use the 22.37% squircle ratio — set with --qa-r-app-icon, not px, so the shape scales correctly at every size.
